About

Hello world! Nice to meet you all!! My name is Sean Rudd. A Florida native from the great city of Stuart, FL. I moved to Orlando, Fl the first time to attend the University of Central Florida where I received my bachelor's in criminal justice. Since then I have moved around quite a bit, just to find myself back in Orlando. I currently work for Moe's Southwest Grill as an area manager and I am taking classes to learn how to code. I am also a proud husband and father. I only play one video game now... League of Legeneds... because I don't have anymore time to buy and play multiple games like I did back in the day. After writting all this stuff down, I realize I definitely need to work on my bio for my real page.
